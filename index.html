About Me

More than just cards.

I’ve always been deep into collecting, gaming, late-night streams, chasing grails, and building communities around the stuff people actually care about.

Pokémon has brought me a ridiculous amount of joy for as long as I can remember. Collecting, trading, gaming, pack openings, vintage cards, grading submissions, all of it.

Flavor & Foils grew from that same passion. What started as loving the hobby turned into building a small community full of people who genuinely enjoy being part of it together.

Some nights we’re ripping packs. Some nights we’re gaming until way too late. Some nights we’re chasing grails, talking submissions, or just hanging out during streams.

No matter what’s going on, the best part has always been the people.

I’m beyond grateful for everyone who’s become part of this little community and helped turn something I’ve loved since childhood into something real.

The Vibe

Built by collectors. Fueled by community.

Flavor & Foils brings together late-night streams, real pulls, collector energy, and community-driven drops all in one place.

What is Flavor & Foils?

A community-driven TCG brand focused on honest cards, real pulls, live reactions, auctions, gaming, collecting, and collector-first energy.

Where do I buy?

TikTok is the main shop and live platform. That is where live sales, drops, streams, and shop updates happen first.

When do you go live?

Live six days a week. Afternoons are usually Tuesday, Wednesday, and Sunday around 3–6 PM. Late nights are Monday, Thursday, and Friday around 11 PM. Thursday is the main auction night. Saturday is the off day.

Do you ship cards safely?

Cards are packed with collector care using sleeves, top loaders, team bags, rigid mailers, boxes for slabs when needed, and tracking when required.

What grading companies do you follow?

PSA, BGS, CGC, and TAG are the main ones talked about for grading, submissions, reveals, and collection updates.

How do I contact you?

Email flavorandfoils@gmail.com for questions, collabs, vendor events, support, or business.
