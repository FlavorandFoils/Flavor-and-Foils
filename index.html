The Vibe

Collector first. Community driven.

Real cards, real pulls, real reactions, and a space where people can enjoy the hobby without all the gatekeeping.

What is Flavor & Foils?

A community-driven TCG brand focused on honest cards, real pulls, live reactions, auctions, gaming, and collector-first energy.

Where do I buy?

TikTok is the main shop and live platform. That is where live sales, drops, streams, and shop updates happen first.

When do you go live?

The normal flow is six days a week with Sunday as the reset day. Exact times can shift, so check TikTok or Discord for updates.

Do you ship cards safely?

Cards are packed with collector care using sleeves, top loaders, team bags, rigid mailers, boxes for slabs when needed, and tracking when required.
